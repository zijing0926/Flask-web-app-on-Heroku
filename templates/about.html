App Introduction

How to predict stock prices? This is the question that frequently asked by all investors. While the long-run market price equilibrium is determined by both demand and supply conditions, the short-run fluctuations of the price are reflections of the market confidence and expectations toward this stock. If we capture these reflections, then we have better chance to predict the price. In my project, I am using the news to capture those reflections by conducting news sentiment analysis, and building a machine learning model.

This app is a machine learning model that will predict the likelihood of crude oil futures price increase. This model will use daily news to capture the market confidence and expectations towards crude oil futures. By conducting a news sentiment analysis, this app will deliver a prediction for any news article for how likely a price increase will follow this news article. Try this model today and become a millionaire tomorrow.
